Vision

Our vision is to become the leading producer of corn, Cassava and Yam in Africa, for onward processing into flour, starch, ethanol and animal feeds, with a minimum annual production of twenty million metric tonnes.

Objectives

  • To acquire and cultivate lands for agriculture purposes.
  • To produce in large quantity Corn, Cassava and Yam.
  • Production of flour, starch, ethanol and animal feeds.
  • To generate employment.
  • To contribute to the standard of living in Nigeria.
  • To contribute to the gross domestic product (GDP) of Nigeria.
  • To contribute to Nigeria export potentials of agriculture produce.
