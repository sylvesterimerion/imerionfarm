Overview

Established in 2019, this company is an agricultural firm that focus on the production of food crops such as maize commonly know as corn ( zee Mays), Cassava and Yam. Located in Edo state, incorporated in December 2019 with the corporate affairs commission with registration number: RC 1641909.

I intend setting up a large scale production factory for the processing of cassava and dry corn sourced from our farms in various locations across edo state into by-products and derivates such as flour, starch, ethanol and animal feeds. I am dedicated to taking this company to greater height.
